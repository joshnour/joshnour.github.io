About

Alibi is an all inclusive NFT provider that helps small businesses develop marketing and loyalty campaigns through the use of NFTs. NFTs have a very high knowledge barrier of entry, and many companies that may benefit from their use may not have the resources or expertise to do so. A local business, such as an event venue, partners with Alibi and we design, mint, and distribute the NFTs for a certain concert they host. The attendees have a verified record of the events through NFTs and can display them on social media.
